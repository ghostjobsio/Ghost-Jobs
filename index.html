How We Score Every Posting

Every job is scored by how long it has been posted. The longer it sits unfilled, the higher the ghost risk.

👻 High Risk

60+ Days Posted

Extremely high chance this role is a ghost. Most legitimate positions are filled within 45 days. This company may be building a pipeline or the role may be indefinitely stalled.

⚠️ Medium Risk

30 to 59 Days Posted

Proceed with caution. The role may already have internal candidates or be stuck in a slow hiring process with no real urgency to fill it externally.

✅ Looks Legit

Under 30 Days Posted

Fresh posting with strong signals of active hiring intent. Apply with confidence — this company is actively working to fill this role right now.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ghost jobs and how this tool works.

What is a ghost job? +
A ghost job is a job posting that a company has no real intention of filling in the near term. Companies post ghost jobs to build passive candidate pipelines, give the appearance of growth, comply with internal HR processes, or simply because they forgot to take down old listings. Studies estimate that up to 1 in 5 job postings are ghost jobs.
How does Ghost Jobs detect them? +
Ghost Jobs uses posting date as the primary signal. Most legitimate openings are filled within 30 to 45 days. When a posting has been sitting for 60 or more days with no update, it is a strong indicator that the role is stalled or was never intended to be filled.
Is Ghost Jobs free to use? +
Yes, completely free. We built this because job seekers deserve better transparency in the hiring process. If you find it useful, you can buy us a coffee — but there is no requirement and no paywall ever.
Where does the job data come from? +
Ghost Jobs pulls live data from the Adzuna Jobs API, which aggregates postings from thousands of employers and job boards across the United States. Data is updated regularly to ensure posting dates are as accurate as possible.
Should I never apply to a high risk job? +
Not necessarily. Ghost Jobs is a signal, not a guarantee. A high risk score means proceed with caution and set realistic expectations. The goal is to help you prioritize your time, not eliminate opportunities entirely.
What is the Company Ghost Score? +
The Company Ghost Score looks at the pattern of postings across an entire organization. If a company consistently has many old, stale listings across multiple departments, that is a red flag that ghost job posting is a habit for them.
How do I report a bug or suggest a feature? +
Use the Contact page to send us a note. We take every report seriously and actively use feedback to improve the tool.
